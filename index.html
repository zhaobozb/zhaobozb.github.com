About Me (CV)

Currently, I am working at the University of British Columbia as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow under the supervision of Prof. Leonid Sigal. Before that, I worked at Disney Research Pittsburgh as an intern for two months. I also spent my last two years in the Learn­ing and Vi­sion Re­search Group at National University of Singapore as a visiting scholar with Prof. Jiashi Feng and Prof. Shuicheng Yan. Besides, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the School of Information Science and Technology, Southwest Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Xiao Wu.
