About

I'm a Technical Lead, ML/AI at BMO AI, based in Toronto, Canada. Prior to that, I worked at the University of British Columbia as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with Prof. Leonid Sigal. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Xiao Wu. I was also lucky to spend the last two years of my Ph.D. in the Learn­ing and Vi­sion Re­search Group at National University of Singapore as a visiting student with Prof. Jiashi Feng and Prof. Shuicheng Yan.
